About eosguide

eosguide is a calm dashboard for noisy money news. We track class actions, refunds, relief programs, and "you might have money waiting" lists, then sort them into one searchable place.

You still visit the official sites to read the fine print and apply. We just cut down the time you spend hunting.

eosguide is an information clearinghouse. We organize and share links to settlements, refunds, and programs but we don't administer them. Always verify details on the official site.

How it works

  1. We collect public information about settlements, refunds, and programs.
  2. We tag each opportunity by category, state, and difficulty.
  3. You search and skim the cards to find what fits your situation.
  4. When you click through, you go straight to the official source.

FAQ

Do you take a cut of any money I get?

No. We never take a cut. If we use affiliate links in the future, we'll label them as sponsored so you can see them clearly.

Are you giving legal or financial advice?

No. We organize information so you can see what might apply to you. For legal, financial, or tax decisions, talk with a professional.

Is everything on the site guaranteed or up to date?

Programs change and everyone's situation is different. We try to stay current, but you should always confirm dates, rules, and details on the official site.

Privacy

We collect as little information as we reasonably can. Right now that mainly means your email address if you join our alerts and basic usage stats from analytics tools.

We use this information to send updates you asked for and to understand which parts of the site are helpful. We don't sell your data. If you ever want us to stop contacting you, you can unsubscribe or reach out and we'll remove you.
