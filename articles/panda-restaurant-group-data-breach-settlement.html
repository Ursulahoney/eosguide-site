    Panda Restaurant Group Data Breach Settlement (PRG) — File a Claim by April 10, 2026

    Panda Restaurant Group agreed to a $2.45 million class action settlement over a March 2023 data breach that may have exposed personal information. Eligible individuals may file a claim for up to $5,000 in documented losses or an estimated $100 cash payment by April 10, 2026.

    _No response_

    TLDR
    - Panda Restaurant Group agreed to a $2.45 million class action settlement over a March 2023 data breach.
    - Eligible individuals may claim up to $5,000 in documented losses, an estimated $100 cash payment, and credit monitoring.
    - The deadline to file a claim is April 10, 2026.

    If you received a notice letter about this incident, it may be worth reviewing your options.

    What Happened

    Panda Restaurant Group (PRG) experienced a data security incident in March 2023.

    An unauthorized third party may have accessed files containing:

    • Full name
    • Date of birth
    • Social Security number

    PRG denies wrongdoing and denies liability. The settlement resolves the claims without a trial.

    The total settlement fund is $2,450,000.

    Who May Qualify

    You may qualify if:

    • You live in the United States
    • Your personal information was accessed or potentially accessed
    • You received a Notice of Data Breach letter

    If you are unsure, contact the Settlement Administrator.

    What You May Receive

    Payments depend on the type of claim you submit.

    If many people file claims, payments may be smaller. If fewer people file, payments may be larger.
