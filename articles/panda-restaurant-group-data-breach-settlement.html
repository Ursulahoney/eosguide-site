What Happened?

In or around March 2023, an unauthorized third party accessed files belonging to Panda Restaurant Group — the parent company of Panda Express, Panda Inn, and Hibachi-San. Those files contained sensitive personal information, including full names, dates of birth, and Social Security numbers.

Affected individuals were notified via letter dated March 7, 2024. A class action lawsuit followed, alleging negligence, breach of implied contract, unjust enrichment, and other claims. Panda Restaurant Group denies wrongdoing but agreed to a $2,450,000 non-reversionary settlement to resolve the case.

Case name: Halliday, et al. v. Panda Restaurant Group, Inc. — No. 24STCV12667 — Superior Court of California, County of Los Angeles. Judge Theresa M. Traber presiding.

What If I Don't Want to Participate?

If you want to keep the right to sue Panda Restaurant Group on your own, you must opt out by March 23, 2026. Mail a letter with your full name, address, phone number, original signature, and a clear statement of intent to be excluded — postmarked by March 23 — to:

PRG Breach Settlement Administrator Attn: Exclusion Request — c/o A.B. Data, Ltd. P.O. Box 173001, Milwaukee, WI 53217

Note: if you submit both a valid objection and a valid opt-out request, only the opt-out will count.

What Counts as a Documented Loss?

Qualifying expenses incurred on or after March 7, 2024 include: credit monitoring or credit report costs, bank fees (overdraft, declined payment, returned check, card cancellation), unauthorized charges not reimbursed by your bank, transportation costs to visit a financial institution due to fraud, costs to obtain credit freezes, long-distance or per-minute phone charges, per-use data charges, and postage or gas for local travel.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need a lawyer to file?

A: No. You can complete the claim form yourself at PRGBreachSettlement.com or by calling 1-866-302-7373.

Q: When will I get paid?

A: The Final Approval Hearing is April 20, 2026. Payments follow after that, and could take more than a year if there are appeals.

Q: What happens if I do nothing?

A: You will not receive any payment and you will give up your right to sue Panda Restaurant Group over this incident.

Q: Can the cash payment amounts change?

A: Yes. The pro rata amounts may go up or down depending on how many total claims are filed and how much remains in the fund after other expenses.

Q: What if I am not sure whether I am included?

A: Call 1-866-302-7373 or email info@PRGBreachSettlement.com and the settlement administrator can confirm your eligibility.
