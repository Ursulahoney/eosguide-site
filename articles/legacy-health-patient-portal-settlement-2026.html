Legacy Health Patient Portal Data Settlement – File a Claim by March 16, 2026!

Legacy Health is settling a class action lawsuit alleging it shared patients' personally identifiable information with Meta (Facebook) and Google without permission — because apparently your medical records weren't private enough. Eligible patients may claim a $15 cash payment and one free year of medical identity monitoring.

What happened

Class period: February 18, 2019 through February 9, 2024

  • Legacy Health allegedly disclosed patients' personally identifiable information to Meta (Facebook) and Google without their permission
  • Plaintiff filed claims for breach of confidence, breach of implied contract, unjust enrichment, and negligence
  • Legacy Health denies all wrongdoing; no court has ruled either way
  • Both sides agreed to settle to avoid the cost and uncertainty of a trial
  • The case is titled Katherine Layman v. Legacy Health, Case No. 25CV40104, in the Circuit Court of Multnomah County, Oregon

Who may qualify

  • Was a patient of Legacy Health residing in the United States
  • Was invited to and registered for a Legacy Health patient portal account between February 18, 2019, and December 31, 2020 (Category 1)
  • OR logged into Legacy Health's Patient Portal between January 1, 2021, and February 9, 2024

How to file

  1. Visit the official settlement website at https://www.pixellegacyhealthsettlement.com/
  2. Click 'Submit a Claim'
  3. Log in using your Unique ID and Claim Login PIN from your mailed or emailed notice
  4. Complete and submit the online claim form
  5. Alternatively, download and mail a paper claim form postmarked by March 16, 2026

Proof required

  • Unique ID from your settlement notice
  • Claim Login PIN from your settlement notice
  • If you lost your notice, contact the administrator at info@PixelLegacyHealthSettlement.com or (833) 647-9039 with your full name and mailing address

Payment timing

Payments will be distributed after the Court grants final approval and any appeals are resolved. The Final Approval Hearing is scheduled for April 16, 2026. Payment method will depend on whether you file online (ACH/electronic) or by mail.

FAQ

Who qualifies for this settlement?
U.S. patients of Legacy Health who registered for its patient portal between February 18, 2019 and December 31, 2020, or who logged into the portal between January 1, 2021 and February 9, 2024.
What can I get from this settlement?
A $15 cash payment (requires a claim) and one free year of CyEx Medical Shield Complete identity and medical monitoring (no claim needed — sent automatically by email).
What is the deadline to file a claim?
March 16, 2026. Claims must be submitted online or postmarked by that date.
Do I need proof to file?
You need the Unique ID and Claim Login PIN from your settlement notice. If you lost it, contact the administrator to request a new one.
What if I do nothing?
You'll miss out on the $15 cash payment — which won't change your life, but it'll buy you a coffee — and if the settlement is finalized, you'll also lose your right to sue Legacy Health over these claims.
How do I opt out?
Mail a written Request for Exclusion including your name, address, phone number, unique ID, a personal signature, the case name and number, and a statement requesting exclusion — postmarked by March 16, 2026.
When will I get paid?
After the Court grants final approval and any appeals are resolved. The Final Approval Hearing is April 16, 2026.
