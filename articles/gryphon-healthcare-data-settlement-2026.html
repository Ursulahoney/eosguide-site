Gryphon Healthcare Data Settlement – File by April 16, 2026

If your personal or medical information was exposed in the July 2024 Gryphon Healthcare cyberattack, you may be entitled to up to $5,000 in reimbursement or a $100 cash payment — plus two years of medical identity theft protection.

What happened

Class period: Early July 2024 (date of the Data Incident)

  • In early July 2024, a targeted cyberattack struck a business associate for which Gryphon Healthcare, LLC provides medical billing services
  • Certain files were accessed that may have contained names, dates of birth, addresses, Social Security numbers, diagnosis information, health insurance information, medical treatment and prescription information, and medical record numbers — basically, the stuff you'd least want a stranger to read
  • A class action lawsuit was filed in the District Court for Harris County, Texas: Morris, et al., v. Gryphon Healthcare, LLC, Case No. 2024-77384
  • Gryphon denies wrongdoing; the parties agreed to settle to avoid the costs and uncertainty of trial

Who may qualify

  • Received a breach notification letter from Gryphon Healthcare
  • Are a U.S. resident whose personal information was potentially exposed in the July 2024 Data Incident
  • Are not Gryphon Healthcare, one of its officers or directors, or the presiding judge (sorry, your honor)

How to file

  1. Visit the official claim form at https://gryphonhealthcaredatasettlement.com/form/claim
  2. Log in using the Unique ID and PIN from your breach notification letter (lost it? Email the administrator to request a new one)
  3. Choose your benefit: Out-of-Pocket Losses reimbursement or the $100 Cash Payment
  4. Complete and submit your form online by April 16, 2026, or mail it with a postmark no later than April 16, 2026

Proof required

  • Out-of-Pocket Losses: Bank statements, receipts, or invoices showing expenses tied to the Data Incident
  • Cash Payment option: No proof required
  • Note: Personal notes alone are not sufficient for the out-of-pocket loss claim — they must support other documentation

Payment timing

Payments will be distributed after the Court grants final approval at the August 31, 2026 hearing and after any appeals are resolved. Specific payment method not yet announced.

FAQ

Who is included in the Settlement?
All U.S. residents whose personal information was potentially exposed in the July 2024 Gryphon Healthcare Data Incident, including anyone who received a breach notification letter.
Do I need proof to file a claim?
Only if you're claiming Out-of-Pocket Losses (up to $5,000). If you choose the $100 Cash Payment option, no documentation is required.
What if I can't find my Unique ID and PIN?
Contact the Settlement Administrator by email at info@GryphonHealthcareDataSettlement.com and provide your full name and mailing address to request your credentials.
What happens if I do nothing?
You will not receive any benefits, and you will give up your right to sue Gryphon Healthcare related to the issues in this case.
When will I get paid?
Payments will be issued after the Court grants final approval at the August 31, 2026 hearing and after any appeals are resolved. No specific payment method has been announced yet.
Can I opt out of the Settlement?
Yes. To opt out, mail a written Request for Exclusion to the Settlement Administrator, postmarked no later than March 17, 2026. If you opt out, you will not receive Settlement benefits but retain the right to sue Gryphon on your own.
